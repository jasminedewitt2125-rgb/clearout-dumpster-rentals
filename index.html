WHAT CAN GO IN A DUMPSTER?

Most everyday cleanup debris is no problem. A few things need approval first because of disposal rules or extra fees.

Good to load.

  • Garage, basement, and attic cleanouts
  • Yard waste and small branches
  • Remodeling and construction debris
  • Roofing shingles and materials
  • Wood, drywall, flooring, and carpet

ASK US FIRST

May need approval.

  • Tires and vehicle batteries
  • Paint, oil, chemicals, or fuel
  • TVs, computers, and electronics
  • Refrigerators and certain appliances
  • Concrete, dirt, brick, or heavy material
  • Anything you are unsure about

COMMON QUESTIONS

Before you rent.

A few quick answers to help you plan your cleanup or project.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Most rentals include a standard rental period. Need extra time? Ask us when you book and we’ll go over your options.

Will it damage my driveway?

Driveway protection boards are available on request. Let us know before delivery if you would like them used.

Do I need to be home for delivery?

Not always. As long as the spot is accessible and you tell us where to place it, we can usually deliver while you are away.

Can I get same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available when trucks and dumpsters are open. Call or text early for the best chance of getting one that day.
